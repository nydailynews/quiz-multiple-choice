QUIZ: Can you match Game of Thrones characters to their deaths?

  • facebook

If you're a fan of the hit HBO drama, you already know that when you play the game of thrones, you win or you die. And a LOT of people have died. More than 1,200, in fact. Some we barely knew and others we knew so well that tears were shed when they perished. Even though a few of them came back from the dead, there were many we couldn't wait to see die in some gloriously horrible way. Superfans remember each and every one. Can you identify who died how?
