Do you know what a demonym is? It's a fancy word for a nickname for people who live in a particular place. If you want to get technical, it's a word that identifies residents or natives of a particular place, which is derived from the name of that particular place.

The people from the Greek island of Crete are Cretans (not to be confused with cretins). The people of Sicily? Sicilians. You get it. Each U.S. state or territory's residents have a nickname. Can you guess what they are?
